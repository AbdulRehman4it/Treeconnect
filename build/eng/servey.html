Maturity Audit

Why this audit?

At TreeConnect, we believe that a high-performing company relies as much on solid internal structure as on the tools it uses. This audit helps you take a clear, honest, and practical look at how your business operates across five essential areas:

• Human Resources

• Administration & Back Office

• IT & Cybersecurity

• Accounting & Finance

• Marketing & Communication

This audit is provided free of charge to help you:

• Take a step back and reflect on your organization

• Quickly identify your key areas for improvement

• Gain immediately actionable insights

You can complete it independently, without registration or any required contact.

How does the audit work?

This audit allows you to assess your company’s maturity across five core areas:

1. Human Resources

2. Administration & Back Office

3. IT & Cybersecurity

4. Accounting & Finance

5. Marketing & Communication

For each question, choose the answer that best reflects your current situation:

• Yes: The item is in place, operational, and under control

• Partially: It exists, but is not yet fully implemented or consistent

• No: It is not in place yet

For each question, choose the answer that best reflects your current situation:

• Yes = 2 points

• Partially = 1 points

• No = 0 points

Note:

Your results will be visualized as a radar chart, instantly visible at the end of the questionnaire. No data is saved.

Final Line:

This audit is a clear and constructive starting point to identify strengths and development opportunities.

Company Information

Please take a few moments to provide context. This allows us to better understand your company’s profile and tailor the analysis.

Human Resources

This section assesses how your company recruits, organizes, and supports its team. It covers hiring, onboarding, performance reviews, HR tools, and internal guidelines. Well-managed human resources reduce operational friction, legal risks, and costly turnover. They also support productivity, improve work climate, and contribute to long-term stability.

1. Have you defined a clear recruitment and onboarding process (job descriptions, structured interviews, employee integration)?

2. Do you conduct regular performance and feedback meetings with your team members?

3. Do you use any system or tool to manage contracts, payroll, absences, and time tracking?

4. Are compensation, bonuses, and benefits defined transparently and applied consistently?

5. Have you implemented an internal guide or document that clearly explains company policies, processes, and employee rights and duties?

Administration & Back-Office

This section evaluates how your company handles administrative processes, documentation, tools, and internal coordination. A well-structured back office avoids bottlenecks and frees up time for core activities. hen administration is unclear or scattered, delays, errors, and stress increase. Clear processes, on the other hand, support team autonomy, client satisfaction, and business continuity.

1. Have you documented your key internal processes (e.g. client setup, contract follow-up, supplier management)?

2. Do you use digital tools to automate recurring tasks (reminders, document sending, archiving)?

3. Do you have a centralized and secure system for organizing and retrieving your essential documents?

4. Have you identified and addressed internal friction points (validation delays, miscommunication, follow-ups)?

5. Do you use a dashboard or system to monitor administrative tasks and key deadlines (contracts, taxes, renewals, etc.)?

IT & Cybersecurity

This section evaluates whether your IT environment is structured, maintained, and aligned with your business needs. It includes your devices, shared folders, messaging tools, website, backups, and digital security.A well-managed digital setup supports daily operations, prevents downtime, protects data, and strengthens client confidence. It also enables innovation and scalability when supported by the right tools and guidance.

1. Do you have a reliable setup for maintaining your IT infrastructure (hardware, software, updates, support)?

2. Are your company’s data securely and automatically backed up on an external cloud, with a recovery plan?

3. Do your main digital tools (email, file sharing, project tracking, collaboration) enhance team efficiency and client interaction?

4. Are you supported in selecting and deploying digital solutions (ERP, CRM, automation, AI tools, collaborative platforms)?

5. Are basic cybersecurity practices (passwords, access rights, confidentiality) understood and applied within your team?

Accounting & Finance

This section evaluates your ability to monitor, understand, and manage financial aspects of your business. It includes budgeting, cash flow, forecasting, automation, and financial tools. Good financial oversight helps secure cash flow, prevent surprises, and support informed decisions. It also increases investor and stakeholder confidence

1. Do you have a clear financial dashboard updated regularly with your key indicators (cash flow, margins, expenses, forecasts)?

2. Are your invoicing, payment, and collection processes well-structured to avoid errors or delays?

3. Do you regularly analyze your expenses to identify areas for optimization or savings?

4. Do you anticipate sensitive periods (seasonality, investments) using adapted forecasting tools?

5. Do you use digital tools to simplify financial management (invoicing, bank syncing, accounting exports, automation)?

Marketing & Communication

This section assesses how your company communicates its value, defines its image, and reaches its target audience. It covers strategy, brand identity, tools, and content planning.Strong marketing increases visibility, credibility, and client acquisition. It requires consistent messaging, professional assets, and smart use of communication channels.

1. Have you defined a clear communication strategy with goals, target audiences, messages, and an editorial calendar?

2. Is your visual identity (logo, website, materials) professional and consistent with your positioning?

3. Do you use the right communication channels (social media, email, SEO, events, referrals) to reach your ideal clients?

4. Do you measure the results of your marketing actions (traffic, leads, conversions, awareness)?

5. Is your communication regular and planned, helping build long-term visibility and brand strength?
